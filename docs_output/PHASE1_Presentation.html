Smart Logistics & Transportation Management System (SLTMS)


A Java-based software application that digitizes and automates all core logistics operations.

🏢

Case Study

Impano Gateway Logistics Ltd


A Rwandan logistics company providing freight forwarding, cargo tracking, fleet management, and last-mile delivery across Rwanda and East Africa.

Problem: The company currently uses manual processes — phone calls, paper records, and spreadsheets — causing delays, lost shipments, billing errors, and poor customer service.

# Problem Impact

Activity Diagram — Shipment Booking Process
[START] | v [Client Requests Shipment] | v [Validate Client Info] | [Valid?]--NO-->[Show Error] | YES v [Create Shipment Record] | v [Check Available Vehicle] | [Available?]--NO-->[Notify Delay → PENDING] | YES v [Assign Vehicle & Driver] | v [Calculate Shipping Cost] (Strategy Pattern) | v [Generate Invoice] | v [Driver Picks Up Cargo] | v [Status: IN_TRANSIT] | v [Deliver to Destination] | v [Status: DELIVERED] | v [Mark Invoice as Paid] | v [END]

🔄 Status Flow

PENDING → ASSIGNED → IN_TRANSIT → DELIVERED

⚡ Auto Actions

• Driver auto-assigned
• Vehicle auto-assigned
• Cost auto-calculated
• Invoice auto-generated

🔓 Resource Release

When DELIVERED or CANCELLED, driver and vehicle are automatically freed for new shipments.
